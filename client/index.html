Introducing the Party

Paladin: Lenora Shane

  • Alignment: Lawful Good
  • Gender: Female
  • Faith: Champion of Chauntea

Lenora Shane is a noble who is originally from the 10 towns area. Her mother, Duvessa Shane, is the speaker of Brynshandr. Lenora Shane is the epitome of chivalry and morality. She will always stop to help those in need. A copy of her Paladin's oath can be found pounded in by her armored fist on the message board of most towns in the Region. You can see her gleaming armor leading the way into battle and water boils under her divine smite's. This is a warning to all ne'er-do-wells... if you see Miss Shane...

Rogue: Dillion

  • Alignment: Neutral Good
  • Gender: Male
  • Faith: Himself

Born as heir to the Reghed Tiger Tribe in the Icewind Dale, born Eluf Koroldsson, his mother attempted to sacrifice him to the goddess of winter by abandoning him on an iceberg left at sea. He was rescued by the Goliath Werebear Oyaminartok and taught the ways of the wild. Alone in the wastes of ice, his burning hatred for his mother and the evil goddess Auril he sets off to destroy them and anyone else in his way.
